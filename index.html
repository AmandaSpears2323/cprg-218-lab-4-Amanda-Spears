Zoo Animals

Hippo

A Hippo or Hippopotamus is a large mammal who can swim up to 30km/h!

More Zoo Animals

Penguins are flightless birds that live almost exclusivley in the Southern Hemisphere. Depending on the type of Penguin they can live up to 20 years!

Gorillas are able to live up to 40 years old and depending on the region the Gorilla comes from they can have a speed of 40km/h!

Polar Bears are native to the Artic, they can run up to 40km/h and is the largest species of bear. Adult males weigh from 300-800kg or 660-1700lbs!
